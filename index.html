Tourist Spots

Discover amazing locations in Butuan City

National Museum - Butuan

A museum showcasing the rich history of Butuan.

Balangay Shrine Museum

Home to ancient Balangay boats, dating back to 320 AD.

Mount Mayapay

A prominent mountain offering hiking and scenic views.

Bood Promontory Eco Park

Site of the first Catholic Mass in Mindanao, with panoramic views.

Agusan River

The longest river in Mindanao, offering river cruises and biodiversity.

Delta Discovery Park

An eco-adventure park with zip-lining, ATV rides, and camping.

Guingona Park

A historical park and popular cultural gathering spot in Butuan.

Butuan City Hall Complex

A notable landmark and the seat of the local government.

Magellan's Anchorage

The site where Magellan anchored during his 1521 expedition.

Banza Church Ruins

It is the oldest stone church ruin in the entire island of Mindanao.

St. Joseph Cathedral

A beautiful cathedral and one of the oldest structures in Butuan.
